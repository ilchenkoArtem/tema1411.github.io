Celebrate the end of the year in the magnificent Villa Castelbarco in Vaprio d’Adda region, in close proximity to Milan!

Castelbarco Villa was built in the 18th century and is a popular choice for New Year’s Eve in Milan, for an evening of luxury in a historic location, with a wonderful gala dinner and entertainment until the early hours of the next day.

A refined menu is proposed. All dishes are prepared with the finest ingredients. Only the best quality meats and fresh fish are served with the finest seasonal vegetables. An excellent wine list is proposed with a wide variety of wines and Champagne to perfectly pair with every dish.

Guests can choose to spend the night at Villa Castelbarco’s 4 star hotel. All rooms are comfortable and fully equipped.
